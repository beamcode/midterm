Japanese Experience

Published 17 May 2023


Why did we choose Japan?


Japan's influence over the other countries is growing fast, for example, as time goes on, more and more Japanese restaurants like sushi restaurants open in Europe or America, more mangas are being translated in other languages than japanese and being sold in regular bookstores.
All of these factors together make that all members of our group were influenced by japanese culture and, as we had the chance to study abroad, we choose to study in Japan.


What we like in Japan


  • Japanese food: Before tavelling to Japan, we all tasted to Japanese dishes such as sushis or ramens, but here, everything is so much tastier and cheaper, it's awesome! And there are far more Japanese specialities than I would have thought (curry, yakitori, yakuniku, melon pan, mochi, daikufu, dango, tempura, ...).
  • Konbinis: They're so useful, it's amazing. You can find everyting there at a cheap price. Moreover they're always open and they are everywhere.
  • IC cards (pasmo, suica, ...): An other handy thing, it acts as your bus ticket but also as little credit card. It's surprising to see in how much stores you can pay with it.
  • Cost of life: Everything is cheap!

What we don't like in Japan


  • Language barrier: Only a few people here speak English well here, and as we don't talk Japanese neither, it's sometimes hard to understand each other.
  • Earthquakes: Apart from being very dangerous when they're strong, they wake you up at 4am and make things at height fall down.
  • No trashcans: It's annoying.
  • Politicians in election period: They drive in the street with their megaphone and wake you up early, it's annoying too.

Our opinion on Japan


Apart from the few small downsides which are greatly overweight by the upsides, Japan is great!

France Culture

Published 17 May 2023

Art and Literature

France has a rich artistic and literary heritage that has shaped the cultural landscape of the world. From the works of iconic artists like Monet and Renoir to literary masterpieces by authors like Victor Hugo and Marcel Proust, French art and literature continue to inspire and captivate audiences.

Cuisine and Wine

French cuisine is renowned for its sophistication and exquisite flavors. From the delicate pastries of patisseries to the rich and flavorful cheeses, each dish is a culinary delight. France is also known for its world-class wines, with regions like Bordeaux and Burgundy producing some of the finest vintages in the world.

Fashion and Style

France has long been a global trendsetter in the world of fashion. From haute couture fashion houses to stylish street fashion, French style is synonymous with elegance and sophistication. Paris, the fashion capital of the world, hosts renowned fashion events like Paris Fashion Week, attracting designers and fashion enthusiasts from around the globe.

Historical Landmarks

France is home to iconic historical landmarks that showcase its rich history. From the majestic Eiffel Tower in Paris to the ancient Roman amphitheater in Nîmes, each landmark tells a story of France's past. Exploring these architectural wonders allows visitors to immerse themselves in the country's fascinating history.

USA's Culture

Published 17 May 2023

Exploring USA's Culture

Diverse and Vibrant

The United States of America, a country known for its diversity and vibrant culture, offers a unique blend of traditions, customs, and lifestyles. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the Grand Canyon, there is something for everyone to explore and experience.

Melting Pot of Cultures

One of the defining characteristics of American culture is its status as a melting pot, where people from various backgrounds come together to create a rich tapestry of traditions. From Native American influences to contributions from immigrants worldwide, the cultural diversity in the United States is truly remarkable.

Celebrating Diversity

Americans take pride in celebrating their cultural diversity through festivals, parades, and community events. Whether it's the lively Mardi Gras in New Orleans, the vibrant Chinese New Year celebrations in San Francisco's Chinatown, or the spirited Independence Day festivities across the nation, there is always something exciting happening.

Sports and Entertainment

Sports play a significant role in American culture, with popular activities like baseball, basketball, and American football capturing the nation's attention. The USA is also a hub for the entertainment industry, with Hollywood producing blockbuster movies and the music scene showcasing diverse genres and talented artists.

Culinary Delights

When it comes to food, the United States offers a wide array of culinary delights. From mouth-watering burgers and fries to delectable Southern comfort food, from Tex-Mex cuisine to fresh seafood on the coasts, there is no shortage of flavors to indulge in during your visit.

Land of Opportunities

The United States is often referred to as the "Land of Opportunities" due to its reputation as a place where dreams can come true. It has been a magnet for artists, entrepreneurs, and innovators, fostering a spirit of ambition and creativity.

Exploring the culture of the United States is a captivating journey that will leave you with a deep appreciation for its diversity, traditions, and the unique experiences it offers. Whether you're drawn to the iconic landmarks, the vibrant cities, or the warm hospitality of its people, the USA is a destination that never fails to leave a lasting impression.
